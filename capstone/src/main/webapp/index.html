Terms Of Usage

Sudentia is a study tool meant for college students. A place where university Students can come to utilize Flash cards or as we call them Study Sets for specific college courses made by fellow college students around the United States. Studentia was founded by three Google interns dreaming of a centralized place for college study tools.

About

Sudentia is a study tool meant for college students. A place where university Students can come to utilize Flash cards or as we call them Study Sets for specific college courses made by fellow college students around the United States. Studentia was founded by three Google interns dreaming of a centralized place for college study tools. As their project they came together to make Studentia a tool that they wish to have during their college.

Get Started

To get started go to Search to find studySets pertaining to what you're trying to study. Study sets are associated with their creators and their school, one can search by school, title, subject or author.
If you want to contribute to the site and add flashcards for you to study from and for others to utilize use the login button at the top to get contributing.
